Great collection of food for people in need / "Gran Recapte - Banc dels Aliments de les comarques de Girona"

Girona [ 1 Nov 2017 – 30 Dec 2019 ]

Address: XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX 17003 Girona (Spain)

Udg Web: https://www.udg.edu/en/

This is a campaign to collect basic food, so that the most needy people in our vicinity receive food aid. The volunteer’s job is to try to encourage customers who shop at a supermarket to donate food and pick it up, so that it can then be delivered to families in need. I did this work as a volunteer in collaboration with the Montilivi Institute and the Girona Food Bank at the Bon Preu supermarket on Lluis Pericot Avenue (Girona).

Friendly Schools of Telefonica Foundation / "Escuelas Amigas"

Girona [ 12 Sep 2012 – 22 Jun 2013 ]

Address: xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx 17003 Girona (Spain)

Udg Web: https://www.udg.edu/en/

It is an educational initiative that aims at cultural exchange between students from Latin America with educational deficiencies and Spanish students to encourage knowledge, collaboration and respect between pupils and teachers in schools of different nations. The activities are carried out with the use of technologies linked to Web 2.0 platforms (collaborative pages), in order to achieve an active participation of all the protagonists (children, educators and volunteers).
