Strasbourg, France [ 13 Jul 2022 – 22 Jul 2022 ]

Address: 17, Place Saint-Étienne 67000 Strasbourg (France)

This volunteering consisted of taking part in a project called Urban agriculture for socio-professional inclusion of disadvantaged Youth. Girona [ 1 Nov 2017 – 30 Dec 2019 ]

Address: Carrer Palol d'Onyar, 3, 5, 17004 Girona (Spain)

This is a campaign to collect basic food, so that the most needy people in our vicinity receive food aid. The volunteer’s job is to try to encourage customers who shop at a supermarket to donate food and pick it up, so that it can then be delivered to families in need.
